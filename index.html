New York City Musicians to the World

7:00pm Saturday, March 28th - 7:00pm Sunday, March 29th

The Corona virus crisis has changed all of our lives drastically. All continents are fighting the pandemic and one result of the necessary precaution is the complete shut down of the cultural life. New York City is a musical melting pot, with a wide range of artists living and working here. I wanted to unite all my artists friends and colleagues in New York City to send a message of courage, empathy, friendship and love to the world. Please join us to celebrate music and joy during hard times!

Yours,
Jan Vogler
